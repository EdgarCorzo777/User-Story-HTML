My Pets

Dog

The Shiba Inu is an ancient Japanese breed, originally bred for hunting. It is independent, loyal and alert, with a fox-like appearance and a characteristic curled tail.

Pig

The pig is an intelligent and sociable domestic mammal. Despite its reputation, it is a very clean and curious animal with a great capacity for learning.

Turtle

Turtles are ancient reptiles that carry their shell as a protective shield. They can live for decades and are known for their calm and extraordinary longevity.

Cat

The domestic cat is an independent and charismatic feline. It is known for its agility, relaxing purring and unique, unpredictable personality.

Rabbit

The rabbit is a herbivorous mammal with long ears and agile movements. It is an affectionate and calm pet, ideal for small spaces.

Hamster

The hamster is a small nocturnal rodent known for storing food in its cheeks. It is a curious, easy-to-care-for pet that is very active at night.
